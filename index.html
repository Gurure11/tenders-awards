Upload files to analyse
Choose a Zoho approvals export, a bank/fuel swipe export, or both. Then click Analyse. The dashboard uploads only those files to Google Drive via Apps Script, stores parsed rows in Google Sheets, pulls driver transactions directly from the Fuel Log sheet in Google Sheets, and produces the reports automatically. No driver log upload is required.
